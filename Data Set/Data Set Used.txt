The project utilized a dataset compiled from real-world sources to differentiate between genuine and fake news articles.

Genuine News Articles: The true news articles were collected by scraping content from Reuters.com, a reputable news organization.

Fake News Articles: The fake news articles were gathered from various unreliable platforms that were identified by Politifact and Wikipedia.

Content Focus: The majority of the articles in the dataset are centered around politics and world news.

Download Link: The dataset is publicly available and can be downloaded from Kaggle at the following link: www.kaggle.com/datasets/emineyetm/fake-news-detection-datasets.
